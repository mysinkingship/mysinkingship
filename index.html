What is My Sinking Ship

Recent socio-economic and political events in Malaysia have registered strong negative sentiments in the general Malaysian population. We have, through various avenues, identified a substantial increase in the desire of ordinary Malaysians to abandon ship and emigrate in search of greener pastures. My Sinking Ship aims to lower the information barrier for immigration resources and empower Malaysians to identify innovative means of escape. Let's start sailing, all aboard!

How does it work?

My Sinking Ship will primarily be built upon a comprehensive, one-stop, curated list of immigration resources for various high-interest countries including, but not limited to, the United States, Canada and Australia. These resources will be tailored to a Malaysian context for maximum impact. We will eventually have translations in the 3 major languages, Malay, English and Mandarin for improving accessbility. Further work might involve consultation services with Malaysians or former Malaysian citizens living abroad interested in contributing their experience.

Is it FREE?

Yes, all resources will be free. We are not immigration consultations or lawyers or anything of that sort. This project is being built out of personal interests. The site will be ad-supported if necessary to cover server, domain and hosting costs.
